# Elevator Pitch – Glimpse
  
**Genre:** Edutainment Visual Novel RPG  
**Playtime:** 2 hours | **Platforms:** PC (itch.io) | **Engine:** Ren'Py  
**Target Release:** April 05, 2026 

(Draft) Hop inside the command pod, control satellites and galactic starships, at the cost of your DNA stripping apart and the virtual bleeding into reality. Ground yourself in those around you, find comfort, hostility or even love. The outcome, inspired by real science and what it takes to launch an endeavor of this size, to really 'save the world'.

**Core Loop:** Play as a Command Pod pilot, control satellite propolsion to starship anti-matter engines collecting real world science lessons to solve problems inside the sim and on fantastical foreign worlds. Then, outside the SIM, gather companions for their stories and unlock alternate end paths and ultimately survive in a cut-throat science mission.

*Play it free on itch.io – adventure is yours to discover and learn.*
