# Elevator Pitch – Glimpse
  
**Genre:** Edutainment Visual Novel RPG  
**Playtime:** 2 hours | **Platforms:** PC (itch.io) | **Engine:** Ren'Py  
**Target Release:** April 05, 2026 

(Draft) Wake up explorer. You need to resurrect the ship, you and your crew have arrived to Exodus VII. Chart the course to land, navigate relationships with the crew, survive in the perils of space, discover the fantastical planets and otherwordly beings.    

Alarm Red, oxygen falling, iminent power failure. Use real concepts in an entertaining and fantastical setting to understand navigating the perils of space and what it takes to survive in one of the harshest environments of the known universe. Gather companions, learn equations, use real world rocket science to explore this fantastical universe of aliens, robots and everything of the *in-between*.

**Core Loop:** Play as an astronaut navigating a spaceship collecting real world science lessons to solve problems onboard the ship and on foreign worlds, gather companions for their stories and unlock alternate paths, and ultimately survive for as long as you can.

*Play it free on itch.io – adventure is yours to discover and learn.*
