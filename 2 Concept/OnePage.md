# One-Page Concept – Glimpse

**Working Title**
Glimpse  

**Logline**  
Red strobbing lights, alarms screaming...no, wait...that was just a dream. Wake up, the orbital scans are coming in. Solar flare is coming in about two hours, nothing serious, need to uplink on the Sim POD and close up the panels for the satellite. But first, coffee. 

**Genre & Tone**  
Silent Protagonist x Soft/Loud mood swings
Noir, Silence, Scenic, Companions bring different themes

**Core Fantasy**  
Adrift, Survival, Freedom of choice

**Target Length & Scope** (locked for 90 days)  
∼2 hours first playthrough  
∼70 000 words  
4 major endings + 3 minor epilogues  
5 locations  
6 speaking characters (3 with full sprite sets)  
No voiced lines · Text only  

**Core Gameplay Loop**  
Read → Choose dialogue / action → Solve Puzzle → Immediate consequence + permanent stat growth → Story branches

**Player Stats (4 only — keeps scope tiny)**  
*Learn  Persuade  Fight  Empathy*  
Each levels 0–10 through choices. High stats unlock exclusive scenes, allies, and endings.

**Unique Selling Hooks**  

- Learn real world concepts of space travel  
- Ending screen shows your final choice

**Target Platforms & Price**  
Windows · macOS · Linux · Web  
Free / Name-your-own-price on itch.io  
Optional $3–5 “Supporter Pack” (artbook + OST)

**Engine & Art Style**  
Ren’Py 8.2  
Hand drawn images, digital sketches
Limited animation (rain particles, glitch effects, blinking UI)
